Create. Connect. Share.

The magic of Christmas is in the spreading of love, joy and hope to the world. Pastor Don shared how his family created special memories through a handmade Christmas calendar. This simple craft brought their family together and created opportunities to spread the gift of Jesus to others. This Christmas we encourage you to set aside time daily to connect with your loved ones and your community. Creating a Christmas calendar is not just for families with young kids. This is a great activity for anyone looking to connect with others. Grandparents. Newlyweds. Singles. Neighborhood Groups. Friends. Teams. The point is to create connection and keep the purpose of Christmas as the focal point this season.

Daily Ideas

  • Bake cookies for a neighbor
  • Take a meal to a nearby fire station
  • Tape change and a note to a vending machine
  • Deliver treats to a nearby school
  • Play a board game
  • Clean out closets and donate
  • Take supplies to an animal shelter
  • Do a chore for a family member
  • Plan and cook a meal together
  • Watch a Christmas movie together
